Play Siege the Castle

Proposal Page Documentation

Instructions:

Select the troops you want to send into battle and try to get them through the enemies defenses. Destroy the barricade at the end of the track to move on to the next level. In order to send out troops, you need to collect gold by having troops alive on the track or by destroying a barricade. Money is king here: if you don't have enough gold to spend out any troops, you lose.
